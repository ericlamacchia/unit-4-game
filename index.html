Jewel-o-rama

A random Jewel game for your enjoyment!

You will be given a random number at the start of them game.

There are four jewels, by clicking a jewel you will add a specific amount of points to your total score.

You win this game by matching your total score to a random number, you lose the game if your total score goes above the random number.

The value of each jewel is hidden until you click on it.

Each time the game restarts, the game will change the value of each jewel.

Good luck!!!!
