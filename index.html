Jewel-o-rama!

You will be given a random number at the start og the game.

Below you will find four jewels, when you click on a jewel you will add a specific amount of points to the total score.

You will win the game by matching your amount to the given amount, you will lose the game if your score goes above the given amount.

You will not know the value of the jewel until you click on it.

The given amount will change at the start of each game and each jewel will have a new value.
